Chocolate Cake

This delicious cake can be made using easily available ingredients such as milk, cream, an assortment of powders such as sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda. Follow the simple steps to prepare a cake even without the requirement of an oven!

The cake is suitable for any special occassions or if you feel like eating it because frankly you deserve it.

Ingredients

  • Oil - 80 ml
  • Yogurt - 80 gm
  • Sugar - 120 gm
  • Flour - 150 gm
  • Cocoa Powder - 40 gm
  • Milk - 100 ml
  • Baking Powder - 1 tsp
  • Baking Soda - 1/4 tsp
  • Salt - 1 pinch
  • Vinegar - 1 tsp
  • Coffee Powder - 1.5 tsp
  • Hot water - 1.5 tsp
  • Cream - 200 ml
  • Powdered Sugar - 30 gm

Steps

  1. Take the milk in a bowl and add 1 tsp vinegar
  2. Mix well and set aside for 5-10 min
  3. Take the coffee Powder in another bowl and mix with hot water, set aside
  4. Take a heavy bottom pan and add salt to prepare a makeshift oven
  5. Place a stand in the pan and place cover followed by heating on medium flame for 10 min
  6. Take a 6 inch mould and grease it with oil before keeping it aside
  7. Take the oil and mix with yogurt and powdered sugar to form cake batter
  8. Mix it well to obtain a creamy texture and then place a sieve over the bowl
  9. Add flour,cocoa powder,baking powder,baking soda and salt to the mixture and sift over the sieve to remove lumps
  10. Use a spatula to prepare the mixture and add the prepared coffee
  11. Add the milk and mix the batter slowly to obtain desired consistency
  12. Shift the batter to the mould and tap to remove air pockets
  13. Place the mould in the pan and cook on medium flame for 30-40 min with the lid closed
  14. Use a toothpick on the center of cake.If wet,heat further.
  15. Remove the mould and set it on a rack for cooling.Cover with cloth to prevent it from drying
  16. Take the cream and add a little powdered sugar, cocoa powder. Gently mix and subsequently whip
  17. Slice the cake at the center and add a layer of cream.Cover the entire cake with cream
  18. Place the cake in a fridge for 3-4 hours
  19. Voila!Your cake is ready
